This website explores the surface levels of the ocean, diving deeper to 1000m where you will reach the 'Midnight Zone', a zone which no light reaches and photosynthesis is impossible, inhibiting the growth of plants or other life that relies on the process.

Hover over each animal's name to discover more about them. If you wish to experience the ambiance of the deep sea, feel free to unmute the audio in the bottom left corner...
